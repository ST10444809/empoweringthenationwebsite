Courses

6-week Courses

  1. Child Minding

    Designed to provide individuals with the skills and knowledge to care for children in a safe and nurturing environment.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Child Development
    2. Health and Safety
    3. Nutrition
    4. Behavior Management
    5. Educational Activities
    6. Communication
  2. Cooking

    This course teaches the fundamentals of food preparation, cooking techniques, and kitchen management.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Basic Cooking Techniques
    2. Knife Skills
    3. Kitchen Management
    4. Nutrition and Food Safety
    5. Menu Planning
    6. Baking and Pastry
    7. Presentation and Plating
  3. Garden Maintenance

    This course provides skills to care for and maintain gardens effectively.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Tools and Equipment Use
    2. Lawn Care
    3. Seasonal Maintenance
    4. Irrigation and Water Systems
    5. Planting Techniques
    6. Soil Preparation and Fertilization
    7. Pruning and Trimming
    8. Weed and Pest Control
