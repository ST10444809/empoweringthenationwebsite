Child Minding Course

Welcome to the Child Minding Course! This program is designed for individuals looking to provide quality childcare in a safe and nurturing environment. Participants will learn essential skills to care for children effectively and responsibly.

Course Content

  • Understanding child development stages
  • Safety and emergency procedures
  • Nutrition and meal planning for children
  • Engaging activities and playtime management
  • Behavior management techniques
  • Communication with parents and guardians
  • First aid and CPR for infants and children

Course Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the developmental needs of children at different ages.
  • Implement safety measures and respond to emergencies appropriately.
  • Prepare nutritious meals suitable for children.
  • Plan and facilitate engaging activities that promote learning and fun.
  • Use effective behavior management strategies.
  • Communicate effectively with parents and caregivers.
  • Administer first aid and CPR when necessary.

Course Duration and Schedule

The Child Minding Course is a 6-month fulltime course.
