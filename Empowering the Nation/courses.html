Courses

6-week Courses

  • First Aid

    • A first aid course teaches participants how to respond effectively to various medical emergencies.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Basic Life Support
    2. Wound Care
    3. Fractures and Sprains
    4. Emergency Response
    5. Shock and Fainting
    6. Allergies and Asthma
  • Sewing

    • This course introduces the basics of sewing, fabric handling, and garment making for beginners.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Sewing Machine Basics
    2. Stitching Techniques
    3. Fabric Types
    4. Pattern Reading and Cutting
    5. Hems and Seams
    6. Garment Construction
    7. Alterations
  • Landscaping

    • This course covers the principles of garden design, plant care, and outdoor space management.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Landscape Design
    2. Plant Selection
    3. Hardscaping
    4. Soil and Fertilizer
    5. Lawn Care
    6. Irrigation
    7. Seasonal Maintenance
  • Life Skills

    • This course focuses on essential skills for managing personal and professional challenges.

    Training Program Includes:

    1. Communication Skills
    2. Time Management
    3. Financial Literacy
    4. Problem Solving
    5. Interpersonal Skills
    6. Emotional Intelligence
    7. Health and Wellbeing
