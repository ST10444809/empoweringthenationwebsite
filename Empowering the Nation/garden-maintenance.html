Garden Maintenance Course

Welcome to the Garden Maintenance Course! This program is designed for individuals interested in learning essential gardening skills to maintain and enhance their outdoor spaces.

Course Content

  • Understanding soil types and health
  • Plant selection and care
  • Weeding, pruning, and pest control
  • Watering techniques and irrigation systems
  • Seasonal garden preparation and planting
  • Landscape design basics
  • Maintaining garden tools and equipment

Course Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Identify different soil types and their characteristics.
  • Select appropriate plants for specific environments.
  • Perform essential gardening tasks such as weeding, pruning, and pest management.
  • Implement effective watering and irrigation methods.
  • Plan and prepare gardens for seasonal changes.
  • Create basic landscape designs to enhance outdoor spaces.
  • Maintain and care for gardening tools and equipment.

Course Duration and Schedule

The Garden Maintenance Course is a 6-month fulltime session.
