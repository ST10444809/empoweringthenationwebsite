Sewing Course

Welcome to the Sewing Course! This course is designed for individuals interested in learning the art of sewing, whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your skills. Join us to unlock your creativity and create beautiful handmade items.

Course Content

  • Introduction to sewing tools and materials
  • Understanding patterns and fabric selection
  • Basic sewing techniques and stitches
  • How to use a sewing machine
  • Creating simple garments and accessories
  • Finishing techniques and alterations
  • Tips for troubleshooting common sewing problems

Course Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Identify and use various sewing tools and equipment.
  • Understand how to read and follow sewing patterns.
  • Demonstrate basic sewing techniques and operate a sewing machine.
  • Create simple projects, such as bags or clothing items.
  • Apply finishing techniques to enhance the quality of their work.

Course Duration and Schedule

The Sewing Course is a 6-week fulltime course.
