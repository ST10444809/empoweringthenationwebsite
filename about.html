Who Are We?

Empowering the Nation is a local South African business founded by Precious Radebe in 2018. Our mission is to provide opportunities for less fortunate citizens to enhance their skills, improving their employability and boosting their income potential.

We offer courses for domestic workers and gardeners, with six-week short courses and twelve-week learnerships. Our programs cover essential skills, such as first aid, landscaping, cooking, and child care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals through high-quality skills training that helps them unlock their full potential. We strive to uplift communities by providing essential training and personal development opportunities for those in need.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of skills development programs that make a tangible difference in the lives of South Africans, fostering independence, self-sufficiency, and employment growth for all.
