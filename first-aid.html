First Aid Course

Welcome to the First Aid Course! This course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge to respond effectively to medical emergencies.

Course Content

  • Understanding First Aid principles
  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)
  • Wound care and bandaging techniques
  • Managing burns and shock
  • Responding to choking and breathing difficulties
  • Handling fractures and sprains
  • Emergency response protocols

Course Objectives

  • Recognize emergencies and understand the importance of First Aid.
  • Perform CPR and understand the use of AEDs.
  • Apply appropriate techniques for managing wounds and injuries.
  • Communicate effectively with emergency services.
  • Demonstrate confidence in handling emergency situations.

Course Duration and Schedule

The First Aid Course is a 6-month full-time course.
