About

Mission and Inspiration

Empowering the Nation is a local South African business founded by Precious Radebe in 2018. The initiative stems from her personal experiences growing up, where she saw firsthand how her parents and elderly relatives were not given the opportunity to upskill themselves or follow formal educational qualifications. This inspired her to create a platform to help those from her community who face similar challenges. The training school provides skills development opportunities for domestic workers and gardeners, helping them secure better job prospects and a brighter future.

Services and Benefits

The primary aim of Empowering the Nation is to upskill domestic workers and gardeners, making them more competitive in the job market. By acquiring new skills, they can offer more specialized services to potential employers, allowing them to command higher wages and take greater pride in their work. Moreover, graduates of the programs are equipped with the tools and knowledge to start their own businesses, transforming them into entrepreneurs and leaders within their communities.

Employers also benefit by having their household employees undergo formal skills training. This enables domestic workers and gardeners to offer a wider range of services, providing households with more skilled labor and improving overall service quality. By investing in the education of their employees, employers contribute to their personal growth and help create a more empowered workforce.

Overview

The training upskills domestic workers and gardeners, making them more marketable when seeking employment, enabling them to earn higher wages due to these additional skills. Beyond seeking better employment, graduates are also equipped with the skills to start their own small businesses and become entrepreneurs. Employers increasingly see the value in having their employees upskilled to offer more advanced services, leading to an overall improvement in household management.

Customers, whether employers or employees, can select from a variety of courses offered. They can choose more than one course, and discounts are provided based on the number of courses selected:

  • One course – no discount
  • Two courses – 5% discount
  • Three courses – 10% discount
  • More than three courses – 15% discount

Company Background

Since its inception, Empowering the Nation has successfully trained hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners through both the six-month Learnership Programs and six-week Short Skills Training Programs. These courses empower them to acquire marketable skills, making them more competitive in the job market or giving them the ability to run their own businesses. The six-month programs are spread across 12 weeks, while the short courses last six weeks.

The company is now expanding its reach by developing a web page and mobile app to promote its services. The platform will allow potential customers to request information, receive quotes for selected services, and sign up for various training programs directly online. The platform will serve both employees looking to upskill and employers interested in improving the qualifications of their household staff.

Branding

Precious understands the importance of a professional and polished look for her business. While she does not currently have a logo, she emphasizes that the images, fonts, and colors used on the website should reflect the professional image and purpose of Empowering the Nation. The branding will communicate trust, empowerment, and the skill-building potential the company offers to its clients.
