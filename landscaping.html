Landscaping Course

Welcome to the Landscaping Course! This course is designed for individuals who want to learn the fundamentals of landscaping design and maintenance. Whether you're looking to beautify your own space or start a career in landscaping, this course will equip you with the necessary skills.

Course Content

  • Introduction to landscape design principles
  • Site analysis and preparation
  • Choosing the right plants and materials
  • Planting techniques and care
  • Hardscaping elements: paths, patios, and walls
  • Water features and irrigation systems
  • Maintaining and managing a landscape project

Course Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand and apply landscape design principles.
  • Conduct site analysis to determine the best landscaping solutions.
  • Select appropriate plants and materials for various landscapes.
  • Execute planting techniques that promote healthy growth.
  • Design and install hardscaping elements effectively.
  • Implement irrigation systems to enhance sustainability.

Course Duration and Schedule

The Landscaping Course is a 6-month fulltime course.
